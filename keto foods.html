Foods that are Allowed

1. Healthy Fats: Olive oil, coconut oil, butter, avocado oil
2. Proteins: Meat, poultry, fish, eggs
3. Low-Carb Vegetables: Leafy greens, broccoli, cauliflower
4. Dairy: High fat cheese, heavy cream
5. Nuts and Seeds: Almonds, walnuts, flaxseeds
6. Avocados: Whole avocados or freshly made guacamole
7. Low-Carb Sweeteners: Stevia, erythritol
8. Berries: Raspberries, blackberries in moderation
9. Condiments: Salt, pepper, herbs, and spices

Foods to Avoid

1. Sugary foods: Sweets, sodas, fruit juices
2. Grains and starches: Bread, pasta, rice, cereals
3. High-carb fruits: Bananas, oranges, apples
4. Legumes: Beans, lentils, chickpeas
5. Root vegetables: Potatoes, sweet potatoes, carrots
6. Unhealthy fats: Processed vegetable oils
7. Alcohol: Beer, wine, liquors with high carb content
8. Diet or low fat products: Often high in carbs
9. Sweetened yogurts
10. Ice cream and other high sugar desserts
11. Most fruits except for portions of berries
12. Snack bars high in sugar and grains
13. Starchy vegetables like peas and corn
14. Honey, syrup, and other sweeteners high in sugar
15. Packaged snacks and processed foods high in carbs and preservatives
